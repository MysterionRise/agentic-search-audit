# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Introduction

Agentic Search Audit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our search quality audit service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
- **Payment Information**: Billing address and payment method (processed by Stripe)
- **Audit Configurations**: Site URLs, search queries, and settings you configure
- **Support Communications**: Messages you send to our support team

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: Features used, audit results, API calls, timestamps
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error reports, performance metrics

### Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication Providers**: If you sign in via OAuth (Google, GitHub)
- **Payment Processor**: Transaction status from Stripe

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Our Service**: Run audits, generate reports, store results
2. **Process Payments**: Handle subscriptions and billing
3. **Improve Our Service**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop features
4. **Communicate**: Send service updates, security alerts, marketing (with consent)
5. **Legal Compliance**: Respond to legal requests, enforce terms

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account Information | Until account deletion |
| Audit Results | 90 days after audit completion |
| Payment Records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Server Logs | 30 days |
| Analytics Data | 24 months |

You can request data deletion at any time via the GDPR portal.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

- **Service Providers**: Hosting (AWS), payments (Stripe), analytics (aggregated only)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger or acquisition

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Export your data in machine-readable format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Objection**: Opt out of certain processing
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke marketing consent

Exercise these rights via:
- API endpoint: `GET /gdpr/export` and `POST /gdpr/delete`
- Email: privacy@example.com

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Incident response procedures

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Essential**: Authentication, security
- **Functional**: Preferences, settings
- **Analytics**: Usage statistics (can be disabled)

Manage cookie preferences in your account settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to servers outside your country. We ensure adequate protection through:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (EU)
- Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.
